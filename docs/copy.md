Voice: warm, plain, short. Talk to the owner like a person. Say what happened and what to do next. English and Spanish below. Replace anything in braces. Keep dish names as they are.

## 1. Transactional emails

### 1.1 Invite, set your password
EN
Subject: Your Plato menu account is ready
Hi {owner_name}, your account for {restaurant} is set up. Tap the button to set your password and see your dashboard.
Button: Set my password
This link works for 24 hours. If it expires, reply and we will send a new one.
Plato, video menus for Aruba.

Asunto: Tu cuenta de Plato esta lista
Hola {owner_name}, la cuenta de {restaurant} ya esta lista. Toca el boton para crear tu contrasena y ver tu panel.

### Owner dashboard
EN: Your menu, Edit menu, Mark sold out, Back in stock, Add today's special, Preview as a diner, Share my menu, Download my QR, Request a change, Views this month, Top dishes, Your link.
ES: Tu menu, Editar menu, Marcar agotado, De vuelta en stock, Agregar especial de hoy, Ver como cliente, Compartir mi menu, Descargar mi QR, Pedir un cambio, Visitas este mes, Platos top, Tu enlace.

ES: Estamos creando tu menu con tu sesion de fotos. Estara en linea pronto. Aun no hay visitas, comparte tu enlace para empezar. Este plato se esta procesando, aparece en un momento.

### Errors
EN: That did not save, try again. This page is not available. Check your link.
ES: No se guardo, intenta de nuevo. Esta pagina no esta disponible. Revisa tu enlace.
